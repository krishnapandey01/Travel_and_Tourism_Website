Egypt

Home to one of the oldest and most advanced ancient civilizations, Egypt stuns at every corner. From thrilling desert safaris to stunning beaches and blue lagoons to the unmissable pyramids, Egypt has something for everybody.

Top places to visit in Egypt

  • Pyramids of Giza

    The last surviving of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, the Pyramids of Giza are one of the world's most recognizable landmarks.

    View More

  • Cruising the Nile

    Egypt is defined by the Nile. For many visitors, a multi-day cruise upon this famed waterway that saw the rise of the Pharaonic era is a highlight of their trip.

    View More

  • Aswan

    Egypt's most tranquil town is Aswan, set upon the winding curves of the Nile.Take the river ferry across to Elephantine Island and stroll the colorful streets of the Nubian villages.

    View More

  • Explore Cairo

    The atmospheric, narrow lanes of the capital's Islamic Cairo district are crammed full of mosques, madrassas, and monuments dating from the Fatimid through to the Mameluke eras.

Things to do in Egypt

  • Explore the great pyramid of Giza

    The last surviving of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, the Pyramids of Giza are one of the world's most recognizable landmarks.the Pyramids of Giza are one

  • Go Scuba Diving at Hurghada

    Spike up your adrenaline level by enjoying scuba diving at Hurghada, a small fishing village in Egypt. You'll meet a lot of colorful fish and corals while diving here.

  • Get a beautiful view from Cairo Tower

    One of the best activities to do in Egypt is having a birds-eye view of the city from Cairo Tower, which is the tallest free-standing structure in North Africa.

  • Hot air balloon ride over Nile river

    Hot Air Balloon Ride over the Nile river should be one of the best things to do in Egypt as it gives you a chance to have a 360-degree view of Egypt's gorgeous landscape.
